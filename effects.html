Effects of Water Pollution

Water pollution has severe consequences on both humans and the environment:

  • Health Issues: Contaminated water spreads diseases such as cholera, dysentery, and typhoid.
  • Loss of Marine Life: Toxic substances reduce oxygen levels, leading to the death of fish and other aquatic organisms.
  • Ecosystem Imbalance: Polluted water disrupts the food chain and natural balance of aquatic ecosystems.
  • Economic Impact: Polluted water affects industries like fishing and tourism, leading to economic losses.
  • Scarcity of Clean Water: Polluting freshwater sources reduces the availability of safe drinking water.

Addressing these effects is crucial for a sustainable future.
