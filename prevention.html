Prevention of Water Pollution

To prevent water pollution, individuals and organizations must take collective action:

  • Reduce Plastic Use: Avoid single-use plastics and promote biodegradable alternatives.
  • Dispose of Hazardous Waste Properly: Chemicals, oils, and medications should not be dumped into drains.
  • Conserve Water: Reducing unnecessary water use helps prevent pollution caused by excessive wastewater.
  • Participate in Clean-up Drives: Engaging in beach and river clean-up activities can remove pollutants.
  • Promote Eco-Friendly Industries: Supporting sustainable businesses encourages cleaner production practices.

Prevention is always better than cure. Small steps taken today can lead to cleaner and healthier water bodies in the future.
